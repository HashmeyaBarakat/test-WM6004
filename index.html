Frequently Asked Questions


Got a question? We’ve got answers! Check out our most common inquiries below





How long does shipping take?

Orders are processed within 2-3 business days. Shipping time varies based on location, but it usually takes 5-7 business days for standard delivery.


Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we ship within Bahrain, but we’re working on expanding internationally soon. Stay tuned for updates!


What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit/debit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment options to make your checkout experience smooth and secure.


How should I store my coffee to keep it fresh?

For the best flavor, store your coffee in an airtight container in a cool, dark place. Avoid keeping it in the fridge or freezer, as moisture can affect the quality.


Do you offer refunds or returns?

Due to the perishable nature of coffee, we do not accept returns. However, if there’s an issue with your order, please contact us, and we’ll make it right!


Are your coffee beans ethically sourced?

Yes! We source our beans from sustainable and ethical farms, ensuring high quality while supporting fair trade practices.
